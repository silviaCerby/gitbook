# Remove a business hub from a collection

**Description:** This article describes how to remove a business hub from a collection.

{% hint style="info" %} **Who can use this feature?** * Workspace**Owners** ,
**Super Admins** , **Admins** , and **Users** * Collection and business hub
**Owners** * Only supported using the Cerby web app {% endhint %}

As a collection and business hub **Owner** , you can remove a business hub
from a collection. When you take this action, you must choose whether to
remove or keep the access that users and teams have to the business hub
through the collection.

To remove a business hub from a collection, you must complete the following
steps using the Cerby web app:

  1. Log in to your [Cerby](https://app.cerby.com/) workspace.

  2. Select the **Collections** option from the left menu. The **Collections** page is displayed.

  3. Click the **Settings** (![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/pc0ldyqu/1752170674/6f9f2e71f2cb190706a437fe11bc/36936d2f-41d9-4370-9fb9-40a7370336bc?expires=1759215750&signature=65f19f04c4b8ec62ff6b5ba6651dc8aa5218a91243b05a6720c77fbc049bf4e5&req=dSciFMh5nYdYXfMU3HP0gAPmosY4xkNyTgsxBdxGOttpRqF67kU%3D%0A)) icon of the corresponding collection card. The collection details page is displayed with the **General** tab activated.

  4. Activate the **Business hubs** tab.

  5. Click the **More options**(![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/pc0ldyqu/1752170948/ef1d7a076f9dcf52566970900e20/e249fe23-65b7-40b9-8984-73f95064b981?expires=1759215750&signature=d1b2bd35ec14f15f642d8b439e20e31da86c02e3d35b5de8b86cee95d75fe6b2&req=dSciFMh5nYhbUfMU3HP0gKBM6TsxauecU4SW3qSPxtxq5RUcXRE%3D%0A)) icon of the corresponding business hub. A drop-down menu is displayed.

  6. Select the **Remove from Collection** option from the drop-down menu. A confirmation dialog box is displayed.

  7. Choose what to do with access granted to the business hub through the collection:

     * Remove access granted through the collection:

       1. Select the **Remove account permissions shared through the collection** option.

     * Keep access granted through the collection as individual access:

       1. Unselect the **Remove account permissions shared through the collection** option.

  8. Click the **Remove from Collection** button. The dialog box closes, and a success message box is displayed.

{% hint style="info" %} **NOTE:** If in step 7 you selected to remove access
granted through the collection, Cerby initiates in the background automation
jobs to remove users from the external app only when users and teams donâ€™t
have other access grants to the business hub. {% endhint %}

Now you are done.
