# Remove a business hub from a collection

{% hint style="info" %}


**Who can use this feature?**

* Workspace**Owners** , **Super Admins** , **Admins** , and **Users**
* Collection and business hub **Owners**
* Only supported using the Cerby web app


{% endhint %}

As a collection and business hub **Owner** , you can remove a business hub from a collection. When you take this action, you must choose whether to remove or keep the access that users and teams have to the business hub through the collection.

To remove a business hub from a collection, you must complete the following steps using the Cerby web app:

1. Log in to your [Cerby](https://app.cerby.com/) workspace.
2. Select the **Collections** option from the left menu. The **Collections** page is displayed.
3. Click the **Settings** (<img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/pc0ldyqu/1752170674/6f9f2e71f2cb190706a437fe11bc/36936d2f-41d9-4370-9fb9-40a7370336bc?expires=1765962000&signature=042d90934618e0a6d31cc5dab6a429a7ef6fdd974508eda55607d12c7564b3bb&req=dSciFMh5nYdYXfMW3Hu4gZ80iGjaS7UCkcCkBkGOro5h2L0m13vqnUtbvPui%0AmQ%3D%3D%0A" alt="">) icon of the corresponding collection card. The collection details page is displayed with the **General** tab activated.
4. Activate the **Business hubs** tab.
5. Click the **More options**(<img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/pc0ldyqu/1752170948/ef1d7a076f9dcf52566970900e20/e249fe23-65b7-40b9-8984-73f95064b981?expires=1765962000&signature=c4d72c2c57e9b11b04c8c8b5853a0b88c1416be0e64bcd04b3df92ea15207008&req=dSciFMh5nYhbUfMW3Hu4gRjaiWi7ES9DjHaLfZevWROjQE08tki7%2Fvk8TBPD%0AbQ%3D%3D%0A" alt="">) icon of the corresponding business hub. A drop-down menu is displayed.
6. Select the **Remove from Collection** option from the drop-down menu. A confirmation dialog box is displayed.
7. Choose what to do with access granted to the business hub through the collection:

   * Remove access granted through the collection:

     1. Select the **Remove account permissions shared through the collection** option.

   * Keep access granted through the collection as individual access:

     1. Unselect the **Remove account permissions shared through the collection** option.
8. Click the **Remove from Collection** button. The dialog box closes, and a success message box is displayed.

Now you are done.
