# Export the entitlement assignments of your IdLCM integration

**Description:** This article describes how to export the entitlement assignments of your IdLCM integration to a CSV file.

**Who can use this feature?**

  * Workspace**Owners, Super Admins,** and**Admins**
  * Only supported using the Cerby web app

  
---  
  
As a workspace **Owner** , **Super** **Admin** , or **Admin** , you can export
and download the complete list of entitlement assignments from your IdLCM
integration as a CSV file.

When you export your entitlement assignments, you receive a detailed snapshot
of your usersâ€™ entitlements, including workspace and integration context,
assignment information, audit history, and user mapping. This export provides
you with an offline record to verify user access, confirm assignment accuracy
against the upstream identity platform, and keep an auditable history of
changes.

To export the entitlement assignments to a CSV file, you must complete the
following steps:

  1. Log in to your [Cerby](https://app.cerby.com/) workspace.

  2. Select the **Integrations** option from the left menu. The **Integrations** page is displayed.

  3. Click the **More options** (![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/pc0ldyqu/1751739402/aa694fbfbc56e929f427d8e93017/fc9e0bb9-f918-4df3-af12-15c4a38ececd?expires=1761858000&signature=f1f3349272efe86cf8661bb2fc7a18963ecdbaf1fe061d06ae9c229d83fd6a24&req=dSciF859lIVfW%2FMW3Hu4gbmL503d1qRFF1SLMQMJgHyZSlSwHEZ4cAx6g6yC%0AlQ%3D%3D%0A)) icon of the corresponding integration. A drop-down menu is displayed.

  4. Select the **Settings** option from the menu. The integration details page is displayed with the **Settings** tab active.

  5. Activate the **Entitlements** tab.

  6. Click the **More options** (![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/pc0ldyqu/1751739402/aa694fbfbc56e929f427d8e93017/fc9e0bb9-f918-4df3-af12-15c4a38ececd?expires=1761858000&signature=f1f3349272efe86cf8661bb2fc7a18963ecdbaf1fe061d06ae9c229d83fd6a24&req=dSciF859lIVfW%2FMW3Hu4gbmL503d1qRFF1SLMQMJgHyZSlSwHEZ4cAx6g6yC%0AlQ%3D%3D%0A)) icon located at the top right of the **Entitlements** section. A drop-down menu is displayed.

  7. Select the **Export all assignments** option. A message box is displayed indicating that your entitlement assignments export is being prepared.  
A success message box is displayed when the export is downloaded.

Now you are done.
