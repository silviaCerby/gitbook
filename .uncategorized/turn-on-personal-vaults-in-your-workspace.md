# Turn on personal vaults in your workspace

{% hint style="info" %}


**Who can use this feature?**

* Workspace **Owners** , **Super Admins** , and **Admins**
* Only supported using the Cerby web app


{% endhint %}

As a workspace **Owner** , **Super Admin** , or **Admin** , you can turn on [personal vaults](https://help.cerby.com/en/articles/13188897-explore-personal-vaults) in your workspace and specify who can use this feature, whether all workspace users or specific users and teams.

By turning on personal vaults, Cerby provides selected users with a secure, private vault to manage their accounts and sensitive information.

To turn on personal vaults in your workspace, you must complete the following steps:

1. Log in to your [Cerby](https://app.cerby.com/) workspace.
2. Select the **Settings** option from the left menu. The **Workspace Configuration** page is displayed with the **General** tab activated.
3. Activate the **Privacy and Security** tab. The **Vault strategy** left tab is activated.
4. Activate the switch in the **Personal vaults** section to turn on the personal vaults for your workspace users. The**Enable personal vault** page is displayed.
5. Configure the scope of the personal vault in your workspace by completing the following steps:
  1. Select an encryption scheme from the**Vault type** drop-down list:

     * **Cloud Vault**

  2. Select the scope of the personal vaults**.** The scope defines who in your workspace can use personal vaults.

     * All users:

       1. Select the **All users in the workspace** option from the **Scope** drop-down list.

     * Specific users and teams:

       1. Select the **Select specific users and teams** option from the **Scope** drop-down list.
       2. Enter a user's or team's name in the **Select users and teams** field. Users or teams matching the name are displayed in a list below the field.
       3. Select the user or team you want to add. The user or team is added to the list below.
       4. Repeat steps **ii** and**iii** as needed to add multiple users and teams.
  3. Click the **Next** button. The**Block email domain** section is displayed.
6. (Optional) Configure the blocked email domains by completing the following steps:
  1. Enter the domain in the **Domain** field.

  2. Click the **Add** button.
  3. Repeat steps **a** and **b** as needed to add multiple email domains.
  4. Click the **Next** button. The**Block apps** section is displayed.
7. (Optional) Configure the blocked apps by completing the following steps:
  1. Select the blocked apps from the **Apps** catalog.
  2. Click the **Done** button.
8. Click the **Enable** button located at the top right of the page. A success message box is displayed.

Now you are done.
