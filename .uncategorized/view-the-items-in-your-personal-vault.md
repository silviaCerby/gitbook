# View the items in your personal vault

{% hint style="info" %}


**Who can use this feature?**

* Workspace **Owners** , **Super Admins** , **Admins,** and**Users**
* Only supported using the Cerby web app


{% endhint %}

As a workspace **Owner** , **Super Admin** , **Admin,** or**User** with a [personal vault](https://help.cerby.com/en/articles/13188897-explore-personal-vaults) turned on, you can view the items stored in your personal vault.

Items stored in your personal vault cannot be shared individually or via collections with other workspace members or teams, and are not accessible by workspace **Admins**. Your accounts and secrets remain private, and only you can access their sensitive details.

You can perform the following actions to view your personal vault items:

* [View your personal accounts](view-the-items-in-your-personal-vault.md#view-your-personal-accounts)
* [View your personal secrets](view-the-items-in-your-personal-vault.md#view-your-personal-secrets)

The following sections detail the steps for each action.

* * *

## View your personal accounts

To view the accounts stored in your personal vault, you must complete the following steps:

  1. Log in to your [Cerby](https://app.cerby.com/) workspace.
  2. Click the drop-down () icon next to your workspace name. A drop-down list is displayed.
  3. Select the **Personal Vault** option from the menu.
  4. Select the **Accounts** option from the left menu. The **Accounts** page is displayed, showing the accounts stored in your personal vault.

Now you are done.

* * *

## View your personal secrets

To view the secrets stored in your personal vault, you must complete the following steps:

  1. Log in to your [Cerby](https://app.cerby.com/) workspace.
  2. Click the drop-down () icon next to your workspace name. A drop-down list is displayed.
  3. Select the **Personal Vault** option from the menu.
  4. Select the **Secrets** option from the left menu. The **Secrets** page is displayed, showing the secrets stored in your personal vault.

Now you are done.
